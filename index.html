Our advantages

Choosing the best delivery method

We develop the best delivery scheme in terms of transit time/shipping costs according to your requirements. It is also possible to combine several shipping methods to optimize delivery times and lower the costs (express, avia cargo, sea transport). It also allows to control the inventory replenishment rates at Amazon warehouses and to optimize storage costs.

Checking the shipment for compliance with Amazon FBA requirements

We ensure the presence of all the necessary labels, barcodes and check the dimensions, weight and quality of the cartons.

Supervising all transportation stages from the pickup point to the Amazon warehouse destination

Our managers provide all the information about the current status of your shipment and give you early warning in case of any delays. We assume full responsibility for customs clearance and negotiations with the transport company in case of damage or loss of the goods.

Cargo insurance

In case the total value of the shipment is considerably higher than the standard reimbursement rates from the transport companies (DHL, UPS, Fedex), we provide additional insurance against damage or loss of the goods.

IT system to process the requests for logistics services

All the key information about the shipment route, tariffs and the required customs documents is stored in our IT system. It grants much higher level of control over the transportation process. Also, the system provides relevant data about tariff rates of different transport companies.
